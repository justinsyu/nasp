> **Key Points**
> - Neurologic
>   - Cardinal indicator of disease: Face of the Giant Panda Sign on MRI in conjunction with motor fluctuations and altered mental status
> - Hepatologic
>   - Imperative diagnostic criteria
>     - Low ceruloplasmin
>     - High serum and urine copper
>     - Fibrosis
> - Keiser Flesher rings may also be present (Ocular deposition)
