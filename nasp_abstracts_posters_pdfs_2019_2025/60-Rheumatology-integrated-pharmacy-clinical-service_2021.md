## Background

* Rheumatology medications are complex, high-risk therapies that may benefit from management with a team-based approach including a pharmacist<sup>1</sup>

* A specialty pharmacy within a health system provides support for medication monitoring, patient education, prior authorizations, financial assistance, dispensing and delivery

* Pharmacists integrated into rheumatology clinics as an extension of the health system specialty pharmacy can collaborate with clinicians to mitigate treatment barriers including medication safety and enhance the continuity of care in the ambulatory setting <sup>2,3</sup>

## Objectives

* Develop an integrated model for ambulatory care pharmacy within rheumatology clinics to facilitate patient education, specialty medication monitoring, adherence, and improve clinical continuity (determined by the number of prescriptions sent to health system pharmacy) with the integrated health system specialty pharmacy

## Discussion

* Within six months of implementation, the number of pharmacist referrals generated exceeded the target goal

* Integration of ambulatory care pharmacists into rheumatology clinics increased clinical continuity of specialty prescriptions

* Achievements demonstrated by the pilot clinic expedited the expansion of pharmacy services to additional clinic sites

* Medication adherence remained high, with a slight increase from baseline

* Integrated pharmacists positively impacted patient care, including medication education, affordability, and safety

* Limitations included ability to extrapolate data, including RAPID3 assessment scores and lab adherence

## Conclusions

* A model for integrating ambulatory care pharmacists within target rheumatology clinics was developed and implemented

* Implementation exceeded target for referred patients by 151%, resulted in a 25% increase in clinical continuity, and an 8% increase in patient adherence, as measured by PDC

## Future Directions

* Expand integrated pharmacist services within the health system to all rheumatology departments and to other specialties such as dermatology

* Optimize data reporting to collect lab monitoring adherence efficiently
