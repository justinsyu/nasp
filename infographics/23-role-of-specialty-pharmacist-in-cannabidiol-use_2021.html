Role of Specialty Pharmacist in Cannabidiol Use

Vanderbilt Specialty Pharmacy examined how an integrated neurology specialty pharmacist supported prescription cannabidiol initiation by managing drug-drug interactions, navigating access, and educating patients and caregivers.
