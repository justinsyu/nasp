Evaluating the Influence of Technology in Specialty Pharmacy Partnerships

A matched-cohort case study compared typical patient management with CRM-enabled smart case coordination across hub and specialty pharmacy settings, focusing on fill rate, time to fill, and partner responsiveness.

Problem

Manual coordination is often slow and reactive, leaving complex access issues unresolved until patients are already delayed.

Intervention

A real-time portal integrated with CRM systems surfaced cases and supported proactive manufacturer-SP collaboration.

Adoption

Across partner SPs, response rates ranged from 77% to 89%, with an overall 81% response within 24 hours.

Outcome

Absolute fill-rate improvements reached 5 points in the SP 15-30 day group and 20 points in the SP 30+ day group.

Implication

Digital coordination appears most useful when cases are delayed, complex, and dependent on rapid cross-team action.

Conclusion: CRM-driven smart case coordination improved real-time collaboration across specialty pharmacies and providers, reducing therapy delays and strengthening patient-centered specialty pharmacy operations.
