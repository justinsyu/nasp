Technology-enabled care pathway

From eligibility to real-time reporting, the pilot turned HRSN screening into a specialty pharmacy workflow

1

Patient eligibility

New and existing neurology clinic patients opted into specialty clinical care services across a 45-day enrollment period.

2

Screening tool

A ten-item HRSN screen was embedded in the Therigy platform and deployed by phone or mobile workflow.

3

Pharmacist interview

Patients with positive HRSN results were interviewed by a pharmacist to identify requested assistance.

4

Social services

Patients requesting help were referred for outreach and connection to local, state, or patient assistance resources.

5

Dashboard reporting

A real-time dashboard aggregated results and generated lists of interventions pending patient interaction.
