Conclusion

Technology made a complex HCV care process auditable

Standardized pharmacy clinical operations supported by a data platform streamlined patient management across clinic collaboration, adherence monitoring, documentation, and longitudinal follow-up. The poster concludes that robust platform capture can improve HCV cure-rate measurement while highlighting the operational importance of SVR12 lab completion and follow-up documentation.
