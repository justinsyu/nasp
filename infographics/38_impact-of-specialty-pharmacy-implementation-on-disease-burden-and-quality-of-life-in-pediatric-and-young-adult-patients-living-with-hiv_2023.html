Impact of Specialty Pharmacy Implementation on Disease Burden and Quality of Life

A St. Jude retrospective analysis evaluated whether a high-touch accredited specialty pharmacy model changed viral load suppression and patient-reported quality of life for young people receiving antiretroviral therapy.

Study mechanics

Define cohort

Conclusion

High-touch pharmacy support filled an engagement gap

The poster concludes that a formalized specialty pharmacy approach created an additional layer of clinical engagement for patients receiving ART, especially while other clinical areas minimized in-person interaction during the COVID-19 pandemic.

No other confounding interventions were identified by the authors as likely adherence drivers.
