Service model

Why place pharmacists inside digestive care?

IBD + viral hepatitis Patients face complex specialty medication regimens, monitoring needs, adherence risks, and prescription procurement barriers.

The poster frames collaborative drug therapy management as the legal and operational bridge for pharmacists to manage mutual patients alongside clinicians in Connecticut.

Implementation pathway

From project plan to measured continuity

Plan and target

Created a comprehensive plan and identified Digestive Health clinics for the initial service launch.

Introduce model

Presented the service concept to clinic providers to define referral routes and expected pharmacist responsibilities.

Authorize practice

Developed and approved a collaborative practice agreement, then formalized workflow in a standard operating procedure.

Operationalize

Trained and credentialed pharmacists, optimized the EMR, and tracked referrals plus prescription capture.
