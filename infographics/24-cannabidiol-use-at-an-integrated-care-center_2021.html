Cannabidiol Use at an Integrated Care Center

Vanderbilt Specialty Pharmacy described real-world prescription cannabidiol use by mapping who started therapy, what prior treatment burden looked like, and how many anti-epileptic drugs remained in place at initiation.

Objective

Describe patient characteristics and medication patterns for prescription CBD.

The study used electronic health records and a specialty pharmacy patient management database to characterize CBD starts managed through the center's integrated specialty pharmacy.
