Evidence Flow

How the abstracts build the case for specialty pharmacy value

1

Identify access friction

Studies track payer approval, first dose timing, limited distribution access, affordability, and patient-assistance pathways.

2

Embed pharmacists in care

Integrated models connect clinic records, prescribing support, patient outreach, and specialty fulfillment into one workflow.

3

Measure adherence precisely

Abstracts stress that raw PMN, PDC, discontinuation, and lapse metrics need chart review and method transparency.

4

Translate into outcomes

Evidence links pharmacy services to SVR documentation, lower hospital burden, treatment initiation, and safety interventions.

5

Adapt under COVID-19

Digital communication and at-home medication programs show how specialty pharmacies extended service models remotely.
