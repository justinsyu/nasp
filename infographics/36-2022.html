Comparing approaches to drive clinical continuity

Yale New Haven Health evaluated whether health-system specialty pharmacy outreach, alone or paired with an EHR Best Practice Advisory, improved prescription capture for specialty medication orders initially routed outside the health system.

Objective and methods

Test whether active HSSP engagement improves continuity.

Objective: determine whether a clinical continuity outreach team, with or without BPA support, improved prescription capture.

Design: retrospective review of specialty medication orders placed in the health-system EHR from October 1, 2020 through May 31, 2022.

Comparison groups: departments with no outreach, HSSP outreach, and HSSP outreach plus BPA messaging.

Conclusion

Continuity improved when clinics were actively connected to specialty pharmacy services.

Clinical continuity improved by approximately 30% for clinics receiving any HSSP outreach, compared with 3% organic growth in no-outreach departments.

The poster concludes that health-system specialty pharmacy outreach, with or without enhanced electronic tools, can improve prescription capture and continuity within a healthcare system.
