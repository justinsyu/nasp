NASP 2023 | Poster 75 | Health system specialty pharmacy

Implementation of a New Patient Case Management System at a Large Health System Specialty Pharmacy

Yale New Haven Health Outpatient Pharmacy Services moved specialty pharmacy care management from an in-house EHR documentation platform to Epic Compass Rose, using cross-functional workgroups to preserve patient care while expanding documentation, reporting, and quality oversight.

Objective

Change the care-management engine with minimal disruption

The project aimed to implement a patient management application that could support specialty conditions, medications, and services across a broad clinical scope while preserving continuity of patient care.

Implementation flow

From build to go-live, with training as the pressure point

Build the foundation

Core Compass Rose build and workflows were completed on target by December 2022.

Validate at scale

Initial and large-scale validation closed on target from December 2022 through February 2023.

Train and educate

Training curriculum, superuser training, end-user training, and clinic education moved later after staff feedback.

Convert and launch

Conversion run-through and go-live shifted from March-April targets to April-May completion.
