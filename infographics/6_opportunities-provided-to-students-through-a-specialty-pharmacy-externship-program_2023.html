Student progression

From pharmacy operations to patient management

Hire and orient

Externs are hired and begin training in pharmacy operations within Wood Street Pharmacy.

Deepen operations

Externs continue working in operational workflows, building familiarity with specialty dispensing.

Enter patient management

Externs begin patient management training in the Clinical Care Center and Patient Access Center.

Apply advanced responsibilities

Externs continue patient management work while preparing for post-graduate specialty pharmacy roles.

Operational workstreams

Three sites convert student effort into specialty pharmacy capacity

WSP

Wood Street Pharmacy

Daily tasks

  • Process and fill medication orders
  • Package medication deliveries for shipping logs
  • Conduct delivery-service handoffs

Projects

  • Validate temperature-controlled shipping
  • Assemble welcome packets for newly initiated patients
CCC

Clinical Care Center

Daily tasks

  • Conduct monthly refill surveys by phone
  • Escalate medication or disease-related issues
  • Schedule medication deliveries

Projects

  • Automate monthly refill surveys through EPIC MyChart
  • Conduct therapy assessments
PAC

Patient Access Center

Daily tasks

  • Conduct benefit verifications
  • Submit prior authorizations
  • Contact insurers for prior authorization status

Projects

  • Capture new patients for SPS enrollment

Quality projects

Externs help formalize and teach the work

Update standard operating procedures

Quality projects include refreshing SOPs that guide specialty pharmacy processes.

Create training materials

Externs also build educational materials that support staff learning and consistency.

Conclusion and future direction

The externship is positioned as a specialty pharmacy talent pipeline

UI Health SPS concluded that the program has provided students exposure to specialty pharmacy over the past 10 years while helping them build clinical, operational, and professional skills.

Refine progression

Continue improving the structured learning experience across P1 through P4 years.

Expand student-driven projects

Create more opportunities for in-depth professional development and project ownership.
