Impact of an Embedded Rheumatology Pharmacist

Shields Health Solutions evaluated whether placing an ambulatory clinical pharmacist inside a rheumatology clinic workflow improved coverage determinations, medication access timing, and provider satisfaction.

Conclusion

Embedded pharmacy support improved medication access operations in rheumatology.

The poster concludes that adding an ambulatory clinical pharmacist to the multidisciplinary rheumatology team can improve provider satisfaction and patient-care quality related to timely medication approvals.

Future work will assess whether faster access and stronger coverage support translate into shorter time to therapy start and better disease-state outcomes.
