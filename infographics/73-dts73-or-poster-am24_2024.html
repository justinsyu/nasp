Conclusion signal Data as access infrastructure

Financial assistance management inside the PMS creates a dataset that can improve access to funds, support proactive procurement when waitlists open, and alert teams when prior assistance is running low or expiring.

Background

Specialty medication costs can drive non-adherence and treatment abandonment, making FA workflows a patient-access lever.

Objective

Analyze success in filing FA applications and quantify savings for uninsured and insured patients.

Methods

Operational data were collected from 26 clinics between May 1, 2023 and April 30, 2024.

Results

FA explorations succeeded 56% of the time, and 65% of patients with an exploration received at least one FA form.

Future use

Data can be stratified by FA type, disease state, income, preferred language, and other PMS fields.
