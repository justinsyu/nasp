Health-System Specialty Pharmacy's Impact on Self-Administration of Denosumab During COVID-19

UChicago Medicine Specialty Pharmacy evaluated how quickly and safely eligible osteoporosis patients could shift from in-clinic denosumab administration to at-home self-administration during the pandemic.

Evidence flow

Eligibility screen

Patients needed prior clinic doses or clinic approval, no prior adverse effects, and ability and willingness to self-inject.

Pharmacy execution

UCM Specialty Pharmacy coordinated fill approval, injection training, delivery, and follow-up documentation.

Conclusion: UCM Specialty Pharmacy rapidly transitioned eligible patients to at-home denosumab self-administration during COVID-19, with a 61% home-initiation rate among approved patients, median 5-day referral-to-arrival turnaround, and no serious adverse events reported.
