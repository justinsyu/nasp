Implementing a vaccine screening protocol for specialty disease modifying anti-rheumatic drugs

Vanderbilt specialty pharmacists embedded a vaccine screening form into drug-specific DMARD counseling notes, turning new-start counseling into a standardized checkpoint for immunization review, vaccine counseling, and documented recommendations.

What worked

Pharmacists described the tool as clear and easy to use

  • The EHR form made vaccine review visible inside the chart.
  • The condensed reference helped pharmacists recommend appropriate vaccines consistently.
  • The protocol fit into an existing specialty pharmacist counseling workflow.

Suggested improvements

Next refinements focused on automation and more complete vaccine context

  • Add COVID vaccine documentation, dose-series options, and declined/completed fields.
  • Pull vaccine dates, lab values, and prior form data directly into the note.
  • Assess long-term follow-up to determine which recommended vaccines are received.
