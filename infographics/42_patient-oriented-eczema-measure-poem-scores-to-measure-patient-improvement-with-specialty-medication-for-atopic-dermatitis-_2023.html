NASP 2023 | Poster 42 | Atopic dermatitis

POEM scores captured rapid patient-reported improvement after specialty pharmacy biologic therapy.

Fairview Pharmacy Services reviewed Patient-Oriented Eczema Measure scores for patients starting dupilumab and found a large early shift from severe baseline symptoms toward lower disease activity within the first 6 months.

Evidence flow

How the poster turned routine assessments into severity trends.

Identify cohort

Patients had an atopic dermatitis diagnosis and were new to biologic therapy between April 2017 and December 2021.

Confirm exposure

Eligible patients filled 2 or more dupilumab prescriptions within a 6-month window.

Capture POEM

Baseline and at least 1 follow-up POEM score were recorded through specialty pharmacy clinical assessment.

Stratify severity

Trends were assessed by baseline category: mild/clear, moderate, severe, and very severe.

Model change

Loess regressions and linear mixed models examined inflection points and average decreases over time.
