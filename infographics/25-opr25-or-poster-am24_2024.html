Operational Pattern

Role clarity reduced redundancy

  • BeforeOncology pharmacists and technicians maintained patient status through manual lists, retrospective reports, clinic schedules, and Epic staff messages.
  • QueueThe automated notification queue surfaced new oral oncolytic prescriptions and prescription status work in a single workflow.
  • TechPharmacy technicians focused on prior authorization, benefits investigation, medication access, patient assistance, and fill status.
  • RPhOncology pharmacists prioritized clinical workup, Medication Therapy Management encounters, and patient education.

Conclusion

A safety workflow became a stewardship workflow

Provider adherence to Beacon Treatment Plans was the critical first step for oral oncolytic safety. The Phase II Epic financial referral queue then standardized capture of new prescriptions at order entry.

By January 2024, the workflow was associated with higher referral capture, a 36% increase in specialty fill volume, and approximately $2 million more monthly revenue versus early 2023.

Ongoing dashboard monitoring was proposed to sustain treatment-plan utilization, medication access support, patient care quality, and financial stewardship.
