Specialty Pharmacy Continuity Optimization

Yale New Haven Health built a multidisciplinary OPS outreach team to reduce fractured specialty prescription fulfillment, engage clinics with low continuity, and reinforce internal specialty pharmacy use through an EHR reminder.

Evidence flow

Methods pattern

Clinic selection came from four practical entry points

Team strategy

Dashboard review identified clinics and providers with high revenue opportunity and low capture.

Clinic request

Clinics requested education or service support, then OPS screened population, opportunity, capture, fill rate, and financial impact.

OPS pharmacist request

Pharmacist-identified education or process issues triggered clinic evaluation and stakeholder outreach.

New drug launch

New drug or formulation availability prompted disease-state and prescribing-opportunity review.

Objective

Build a repeatable continuity process from OPS

Develop, implement, and evaluate a multidisciplinary outreach team based out of OPS specialty pharmacy to enhance specialty clinical continuity across YNHH clinics.

Implementation detail

Continuity was framed as patient experience, not just prescription capture

  • OPS materials described the benefits of integrated specialty pharmacy support.
  • Immediate follow-up aligned expectations and addressed needs or concerns.
  • Staff education supported EHR reminder adoption after authorization.
  • Progress reports analyzed data, reviewed goals, and surfaced new clinic needs.

Conclusion

Targeted outreach improved prescription capture

  • The poster reports the primary outcome was achieved: targeted clinics improved specialty prescription capture compared with baseline.
  • The team built awareness of OPS services, clarified communication with stakeholders, and supported clinicians and patients.
  • Improved continuity was connected to higher-value, more patient-centered access to care.

Barriers and next steps

Adoption required sustained relationship management

Slow prescribing-pattern change
Insurance lockouts
Patient preference
IT limitations

Future work centered on continued outreach and maintenance of current clinic relationships by the multidisciplinary team.
