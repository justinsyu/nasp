NASP 2023 / Health System Specialty Pharmacy

Evaluation of IBD Treatment Discontinuation Rates in Patients within Health System Specialty Pharmacy

A multicenter retrospective analysis followed patients with Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis managed by embedded health system specialty pharmacy pharmacists from October 2019 through February 2023.

At 18 months, therapy discontinuation was 18% in the HSSP-managed cohort, below the 30% national benchmark cited in the poster.

Conclusion

Less than one-fourth of IBD therapies were discontinued among patients managed within the HSSP.

Mechanism proposed

Embedded pharmacists could communicate directly with prescribers and patients while using electronic health record access to identify discontinuation risks early.

Controllable barriers

Cost accounted for only 2% of discontinuation reasons, with pharmacists helping overturn prior authorization denials and secure financial assistance.
