Digital refill channels were associated with higher oncology medication adherence.

AcariaHealth compared proportion of days covered across phone, online portal, and text refill pathways for oncology prescriptions filled from April 1, 2023 through March 31, 2024.

Evidence flow

How the poster converted refill behavior into an adherence signal.

Define cohort

Patients receiving oncology medications from AcariaHealth during the 12-month study window.

Assign channel

Each refill was categorized as phone, online portal, or text messaging.

Calculate PDC

Adherence was measured with the proportion of days covered formula.

Test differences

Phone was compared with portal and text using two-sample t-tests at p<0.05.

Offering more refill options may reduce friction in oncology therapy management.

The poster interprets higher digital-channel PDC as support for technology-enabled refill workflows while noting the need to encourage initial digital enrollment for more balanced future comparisons.

Objective and conclusion

Study question

Objective: Examine how refill channels impact medication adherence among patients receiving oncology medications.

Conclusion: High adherence appeared across all channels, with statistically significant increases for portal and text compared with phone.
