NASP 2021 poster 54

Impact of adding a Community Healthcare Worker to specialty pharmacy care

Walmart Specialty Pharmacy evaluated whether a CHW embedded in an interdisciplinary team could identify social barriers, connect patients to resources, and document intervention patterns across social determinants of health.

Objective

Test whether a CHW expands the specialty team’s ability to act on social barriers

The program used routine clinical touchpoints to surface adherence barriers, depression-screening concerns, and social needs. The CHW then conducted telephonic outreach and recorded the resulting assistance, creating a structured view of where patients most often needed support.

Evidence flow

How patients moved from screening to documented SDOH intervention

Screen

Pharmacists and registered nurses screened patients during medication counseling and therapeutic assessments.

Probe barriers

Clinicians asked about social conditions, including barriers that could affect adherence.

Assess mood risk

Patients also completed PHQ-2 depression screening as part of the intake signal.

Alert CHW

Interview findings, questionnaire responses, and clinician judgment triggered CHW outreach.

Document support

After the call, notes and outcomes were recorded and categorized into six intervention domains.

Methods snapshot

Retrospective, single-center program review

Eligible participants were patients actively filling any prescription with Walmart Specialty Pharmacy who opted into CHW service. No exclusion criteria were reported.

  • Pharmacist counseling and nurse assessments were the screening entry points.
  • CHW outreach was telephonic after referral from the clinical team.
  • Outcomes were classified into six SDOH domains and tracked monthly.

Conclusion

CHW support helped connect patients to resources

The authors concluded that adding a CHW was beneficial for identifying barriers, connecting patients with assistance, and serving as a liaison between patients and the healthcare system. They recommended future work measuring adherence or clinical parameters before and after CHW intervention.
