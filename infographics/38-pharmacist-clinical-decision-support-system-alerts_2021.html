Impact of Pharmacist Clinical Decision Support System Alerts on Pharmacist Interventions

Trellis Rx evaluated how Arbor platform clinical decision support alerts helped specialty pharmacists identify out-of-range medication-specific labs and turn those signals into safety, efficacy, and appropriateness interventions.

Objective

Describe the clinical outcome impact of pharmacist CDSS alerts.

The study reviewed alerts that fired when pre-determined, out-of-range lab values were entered at medication initiation or continuation.

Each alert prompted pharmacist assessment of medication safety, efficacy, and overall appropriateness before a recommendation or outcome was documented.
