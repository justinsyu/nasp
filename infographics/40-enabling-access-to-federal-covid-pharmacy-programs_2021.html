Case study | Federal COVID-19 vaccination access

Enabling Access to Federal COVID-19 Pharmacy Programs

Managed Health Care Associates described how network-administrator advocacy helped post-acute care pharmacies participate at launch in two federal COVID-19 vaccination programs: the long-term care partnership and the retail pharmacy program.

Objective

Document how access was secured at the earliest launch points

The case study reviewed the MHA network administrator experience working with CDC and other stakeholders so post-acute care pharmacies could participate in both federal pharmacy vaccination programs upon launch.

Method

Retrospective activity review

Investigators reviewed communications and advocacy activities with CDC, HHS, jurisdictions, CMS, and pharmacy and long-term care organizations from September 2020 through February 2021.

Evidence flow

From federal planning to pharmacy participation

3Q 2020

CDC began planning to prioritize long-term care residents and staff for vaccine availability.

Sep 2020

MHA review window opened, capturing communications and stakeholder activity.

Dec 21, 2020

Federal Pharmacy Partnership for LTC launched nationally with MHA network pharmacies included.

Feb 2021

Federal Retail Pharmacy Program launched with MHA network participation through selected partners.

Stakeholder bridge

Advocacy linked agencies, jurisdictions, and provider organizations

  • Advocacy focused on including post-acute care pharmacies when programs launched, not only after later expansion.
  • CDC program goals emphasized end-to-end LTC vaccination support and reduced administrative burden for facilities and health departments.
  • Retail pharmacy participation leveraged pharmacists' community access, vaccination training, and patient relationships.

Result

Access at launch

MHA successfully advocated for member pharmacy participation in both federal COVID-19 vaccination programs, with MHA serving as network administrator for participating post-acute care pharmacies.

Conclusion: early, multi-stakeholder advocacy enabled post-acute care pharmacies to participate in both federal COVID-19 pharmacy programs at their respective launches, supporting faster vaccine deployment through the MHA network.
