Impact of clinical dashboards for data capture and reporting across health system specialty pharmacies

Trellis Rx, now part of CPS, evaluated whether clinical dashboards improved clinical outcome measure capture and protocol-driven pharmacist intervention completion across multiple health system specialty pharmacy partner sites.

Objective

Can clinical dashboards improve real-time patient outcome tracking across HSSP partner sites?

Health system specialty pharmacies rely on clinical outcome measures to evaluate disease status, track progress, and identify opportunities for proactive pharmacist intervention.

The quality improvement question was whether a standardized dashboard could move this work from manual review to more reliable, reportable capture across multiple sites.

Discussion and conclusion

Dashboards were presented as an efficient data collection system for HSSP quality reporting.

  • Clinical dashboards supported statistically significant improvements in PROM capture, collected labs/tests, and completed protocol-driven pharmacist interventions.
  • Post-dashboard quarters showed consistently high performance, suggesting the reporting process was maintained after implementation.
  • The authors concluded that dashboards can help pharmacists monitor patient progress in real time and ensure clinical goals are achieved.
