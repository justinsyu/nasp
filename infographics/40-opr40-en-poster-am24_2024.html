Evaluating the safety of anti-amyloid monoclonal antibody infusions in alternative sites of care

Soleo Health reviewed 2023 infusion records to describe nurse-observed infusion reactions, patient or caregiver reported adverse events, and ARIA-related symptoms for anti-amyloid mAb administration in the home and ambulatory infusion centers.

Objective

Describe safety observations for anti-amyloid mAb infusions in alternative care sites, including the home.

Methods

Retrospective medical record review of 30 patients and 194 infusions from January 1 to December 31, 2023.

Primary signal

Infusions were completed without discontinuation for infusion-related reactions.

Monitoring lens

Nurses assessed infusion reactions, reported adverse events, ARIA symptoms, and cognitive status.

Conclusion

Specialty pharmacies may help support safe access to complex Alzheimer therapies in alternative sites of care.
