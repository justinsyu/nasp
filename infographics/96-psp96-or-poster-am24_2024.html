Conclusion

Structured onboarding translated into practical readiness

The enhanced onboarding program was associated with higher call and documentation quality scores from first to last call and faster attainment of in-seat efficiency after transition to workflow.

Background

Specialty pharmacy onboarding must equip pharmacists with consistent competencies despite varied prior work experience.

Intervention

A standardized yet personalized remote program combined self-study, live instruction, shadowing, and patient outreach.

Measurement

Quality was assessed through call and documentation reviews for 29 trainees at the start and end of training.

Comparator

Efficiency timing was compared between prior-program hires from Sep-Nov 2022 and new-program hires from Dec 2022-Dec 2023.

Signal

Final audited scores exceeded 92% in both quality domains, and time to efficiency fell by 30.3 days.
