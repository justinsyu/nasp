Patient insurance experience

Coverage navigation remained a weak point

  • Only 7% said they understood biologic or biosimilar insurance coverage pretty well or very well.
  • 22% said their care team provides a good amount or a lot of support navigating insurance.
  • 21% said plan customer service usually or always provides needed information.

Objective

Identify and address patient and provider barriers to evidence-based biosimilar utilization through educational web apps.

Interactive design

The app used tethered learning pathways, specialty tracks, evidence summaries, animations, counseling videos, and downloadable tools.

Utilization signal

Among patients ever prescribed a biologic, only 15% reported taking a biosimilar, while 69% said no and 16% were not sure.

Provider action plans

Post-education, providers planned to learn approved biosimilars, review data, and discuss options with eligible patients.

Conclusion

Digital education may support shared decision-making, biosimilar knowledge, utilization, and managed care access strategies.
