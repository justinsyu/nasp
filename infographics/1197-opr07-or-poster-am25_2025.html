Methods in brief

Data sources and inclusion lens

  • Data came from the electronic health record, concierge program data, and pharmacist assessments from 2023-2024.
  • PA and FA analyses included all patients serviced, independent of dispensing pharmacy or PMP enrollment.
  • PMP analysis included adults with oncology diagnosis and at least 3 fills of an oral oncolytic at the integrated HSSP.
  • Paired hospitalization analysis used patient-reported outcomes from semiannual assessments.

Conclusion

Access infrastructure plus PMP monitoring formed a replicable oncology model

The poster concludes that comprehensive specialty pharmacy services helped mitigate medication access barriers and were associated with lower reported hospitalizations and fewer ADR-related discontinuations among PMP patients.

Reported limitations included multiple data sources, paired-data availability only for patients with both initial and reassessment data, incomplete retention across the full PMP period, and possible nonprogram drivers of hospitalization results.
