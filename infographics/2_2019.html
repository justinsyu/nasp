Psoriasis patients utilizing secukinumab, ixekizumab, or brodalumab

1. Identify starts

Pharmacy records found adults starting secukinumab, ixekizumab, or brodalumab from January 2016 through December 2017.

2. Split pathways

Patients were classified as first biologic users or switchers from a prior biologic in the previous 12 months.

3. Compare PDC

Adherence was compared before and after switching using adjusted repeated-measures models.

4. Capture reasons

Patient-reported switch reasons were drawn from the clinical management platform where available.

5. Track symptoms

Two consecutive post-switch responses summarized whether symptoms were better, the same, worse, or inconsistent.

Conclusion

IL-17 inhibitors expanded specialty pharmacy treatment options

The authors concluded that recent IL-17 inhibitors gave psoriasis patients additional treatment options when alternative therapy was needed.

By the last quarter of 2017, the rise in first-biologic IL-17 prescribing suggested growing clinical acceptance of the class in psoriasis care.
