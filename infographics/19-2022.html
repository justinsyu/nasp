Conclusion and discussion

  • Proactive MAS review sharply reduced time from prescribing to BI/PA initiation.
  • Earlier access work corresponded with faster PA determination and internal dispensing timelines.
  • The authors noted workflow enhancements and fewer EHR communications across MAS, pharmacy, providers, and clinic staff.
  • Small pilot sample size and payer variability were cited as important limitations.
  • Future opportunities include expanding MSOT to additional specialty clinics and service lines.
