Objective

Test whether an embedded specialty pharmacy model reduced barriers to HCV treatment

The study assessed medication access, affordability, and outcomes for adults receiving an HCV regimen prescribed by a Southern Ohio Medical Center provider with Trellis Rx integrated services.

Conclusion

Access was solved; follow-up became the next frontier

The integrated clinical model achieved full treatment completion, full access, near-zero average out-of-pocket cost after assistance, and 100% documented SVR12 rates across regimens.

Provider satisfaction was high, with an average recommendation likelihood of 8 out of 10, while the authors identified post-therapy SVR12 lab return as the major workflow opportunity.
