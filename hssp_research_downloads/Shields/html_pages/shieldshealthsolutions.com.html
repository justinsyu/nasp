Who We Serve

Our objective is simple: break down barriers between the patient and the specialty medication they critically need.

For Providers

Our solutions address the complexities of specialty by increasing access to payers and drugs, using a high-touch, data-powered model to lower the total cost of care.

For Drug Manufacturers

We serve as your strategic partner, helping optimize the impact of specialty products by expediting patient access to treatment, all while maintaining superior outcomes.

Our Care Model

Leveraging our expertise, proven clinical model and proprietary technology, we elevate what specialty pharmacy can do for health systems.

Enabled by Expertise

Our skilled experts help health systems unlock access to limited distribution drugs, major payers, and PBMs to lower the cost of care and increase access.

Care Teams You Can Trust

Our trained, certified and specialized clinical pharmacists, technicians and centralized resources help patients navigate treatment while alleviating the administrative burden for clinicians.

Data That Delivers Results

Our purpose-built technology platform, TelemetryRx®, helps identify eligible specialty patients and provides customized data reporting from a single, standardized source.
